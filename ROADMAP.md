# Copetti Capital Roadmap

## Vision

Copetti Capital is an AI-powered real estate investment intelligence platform that helps investors identify, analyze, compare, and monitor residential investment opportunities.

The platform combines property data, market intelligence, financial analysis, and artificial intelligence to support better investment decisions.
